Deep Dive: Sentiment Analysis

To understand the "why" behind employee feelings, we built a Deep Learning model to classify text. It reads employee reviews and determines if the sentiment points towards staying or leaving.

  1. Clean & Tokenize: Raw text is cleaned, and words are converted to numerical tokens.
  2. Sequence Padding: All text inputs are standardized to a fixed length for the model.
  3. LSTM Prediction: The LSTM network analyzes the sequence to predict the final sentiment.
